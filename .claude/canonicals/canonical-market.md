# canonical-market.md

# 1. Header (Identity)
- Product name
- Domain
- Last updated
- One-line market position

# 2. Product Definition
- What the product does — one paragraph
- Core promise — one sentence
- Revenue model (subscription, token, one-time, hybrid)

# 3. Target Audience (ICP)
## Primary (alphabetical)
- Segment, role, why they buy
## Secondary (alphabetical)
- Segment, role, why they buy

# 4. Problems Solved
## Pain Points (alphabetical)
- Specific, measurable problems the audience has today
## Value Delivered (alphabetical)
- What changes after they use the product

# 5. Pricing
- Tiers, prices, what each tier includes
- Token allocations if applicable
- Annual vs monthly if applicable

# 6. Competitive Landscape
## Closest Alternatives (alphabetical)
- Name, what they do, where they fall short
## Differentiators
- What this product does that alternatives do not

# 7. Go-to-Market
## Channels (alphabetical)
- Where the audience is found and engaged
## Messaging (alphabetical)
- Key phrases, taglines, positioning statements

# 8. Tone and Voice
- Rules that apply to all copy for this product
- What to do and what to avoid

# 9. Risks and Challenges
## Structural
- Market or product risks
## Competitive
- Competitive threats

# 10. Content Assets
- What content exists or should exist to support GTM

# 11. Ecosystem Context
- How this product fits into the broader platform
- Cross-sell relationships
- Shared infrastructure dependencies

# 12. Cited Sources (if applicable)
- Public sources referenced in market analysis
