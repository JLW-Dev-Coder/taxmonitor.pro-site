# MARKET.md

Tax Monitor Pro — Taxpayer Market & Revenue Model

---

## 1. Market Positioning

Tax Monitor Pro is not software.

It is a structured IRS monitoring service designed for tax professionals to deliver ongoing oversight and early detection for taxpayers with compliance exposure risk.

This product monetizes post-resolution and at-risk segments inside an existing client book.

---

## 2. Addressable Taxpayer Market

We are not targeting the general tax population.

Target profile:

* Individuals with back tax balances
* Installment agreement clients
* Taxpayers with prior compliance issues
* High-income Schedule C earners
* 1099 earners
* Real estate owners
* Gig workers
* Clients with IRS notice history
* Taxpayers with enforcement anxiety

National IRS data consistently shows:

* Tens of millions of taxpayers owe back taxes
* Millions enter installment agreements annually
* Millions receive IRS notices annually

Even in a single metro area, the addressable segment is in the tens of thousands.

Tax Monitor does not require mass market penetration.

It requires 15–30 properly positioned clients.

---

## 3. Buyer Psychology

Monitoring buyers are not:

* Casual W-2 wage earners
* Low-risk filers
* Basic annual return clients

Monitoring buyers are purchasing:

* Visibility
* Early detection
* Authority reinforcement
* Psychological relief
* Ongoing oversight

This is a preventative positioning model.

It is calm.
It is structured.
It is not negotiation-driven.

---

## 4. Internal Market Leverage

This product is primarily:

* An upsell layer
* A backend monetization system
* A follow-up system for prior resolution clients

Example:

Firm with 500 clients
If 5% qualify → 25 monitoring candidates

That alone sustains the service.

No national SEO required.
No paid funnel required.
No volume marketing required.

---

## 5. Revenue Model (Baseline Scenario)

### Assumptions

* Average service price: $425
* New clients per month: 5
* Labor per client per cycle: 2 hours
* Labor valuation: $125/hour

$2,625

~$2,595

Assume setup investment: $2,000

At 5 new clients per month:

Month 1: ~$845
Month 2: ~$845

Setup recovered between Month 2 and Month 3.
