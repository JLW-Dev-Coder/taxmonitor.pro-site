What changed

A transcript hold (code 570) appeared, followed by a notice (code 971). This pattern often means the IRS needs more time to process something or has a mismatch to resolve.

What it likely means
  • There may be a wage/income mismatch or identity verification delay.
  • A notice was generated; the details will be on the letter.
  • Dates matter. We track new entries and changes so you don‚Äôt miss escalation.
Next step (monitoring context)

Watch for the notice details, confirm the notice date, and track whether the hold clears or escalates. If the letter requires a response, we flag it early so you have time to act.
