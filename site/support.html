Support FAQ

The answers that cover most tickets.

Does intake create IRS representation?

No. Monitoring does not create IRS representation. Representation, filing, and resolution are separate engagements.

How long does the first status take?

Your first status is typically delivered within 5 business days after intake is complete and required access steps are satisfied.

Where do I find reports and transcripts?

Reports and transcripts are delivered through your secure portal. If a download fails or a link looks expired, email support and include a screenshot.

Can you interpret a notice for me?

Monitoring reports translate what changed on your IRS account. If you need advice, representation, or resolution, that is handled under a separate engagement.
