Pricing FAQ

Clear expectations, no surprises.

Does Free require a payment method? ⌄
No. Free is a real $0 membership tier intended for dashboard access, directory browsing, and saved matches without paid tokens.
What is included with membership? ⌄
Membership includes dashboard access and plan-based features. Free includes browsing and saved matches, while paid tiers add inquiry access, tokens, and deeper visibility into your tax activity.
Are these tax resolution packages? ⌄
No. These memberships are for software access, discovery, and monthly usage credits. Tax resolution, filing, or representation services are separate engagements with a tax professional.
Can I cancel or change plans? ⌄
Yes. You can start on Free, cancel anytime, and move to a paid membership when you need tokens, inquiry submission, or priority matching.
Do unused tokens expire? ⌄
Unused tokens roll over for 60 days, giving you more flexibility before they expire.
