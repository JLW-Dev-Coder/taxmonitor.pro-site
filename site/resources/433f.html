Form 433‑F, Explained

Use this interactive sample to understand what the IRS is actually asking for and how the numbers connect. You can edit fields, reset everything, and download your filled snapshot.

What it’s for

A Collection Information Statement used to evaluate your ability to pay and what proof they’ll request.

What trips people up

Missing documentation, inconsistent totals, or mixing business and personal numbers.

How to use this page

Work left to right. Enter realistic monthly amounts. Download your snapshot to share with your preparer or advisor.
