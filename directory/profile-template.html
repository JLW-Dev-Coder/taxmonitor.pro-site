About

Sarah Mitchell is a dual-credentialed tax professional with over 20 years of experience in complex tax litigation and IRS audit defense. With both her CPA and law degree, she brings a unique perspective to tax problem-solving.

She has successfully represented clients before the IRS, Tax Court, and various state tax authorities. Her expertise includes navigating complex audit procedures, developing effective defense strategies, and negotiating favorable settlements with tax authorities.

Known for her meticulous attention to detail and aggressive advocacy, Sarah has built a reputation for achieving strong outcomes for clients facing high-stakes tax disputes.
