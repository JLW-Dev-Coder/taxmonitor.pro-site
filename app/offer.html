How It Works

We guide you through a clear, three-stage process to obtain and review your tax records. Each stage is designed to ensure accuracy, compliance, and visibility while keeping the service moving forward in a structured and predictable way.

You will receive check-ins at each stage so you know exactly where things stand and what, if anything, is needed from you.

Stage 1
eSign 2848 & Initial Review

In the first stage, you complete a brief tax organizer and electronically sign Form 2848. This allows us to request access to your tax records from the Internal Revenue Service (IRS).

Once records are received, we perform an initial review to confirm completeness and accuracy before proceeding. This step helps ensure the authorization is properly processed and accepted for Centralized Authorization File (CAF) purposes.

Stage 2
Processing & Due Diligence

During this stage, your signed Form 2848 is processed and added to your CAF record. Processing times typically take up to two weeks, though delays may occur depending on IRS volume.

Once authorization is confirmed, we can access your tax transcripts and monitor for updates or notices during your applicable service period. Status updates are provided at consistent intervals. If electronic authorization cannot be completed, we may request an ink-signed form.

Stage 3
Tax Record Delivery & Review

After your tax records are received, they are compiled and organized within your file for secure access. Any applicable monitoring reports are prepared and made available for download.

You may schedule a review meeting to discuss your records, clarify findings, and understand next steps. Completion of this stage confirms successful delivery of your tax monitoring service.
