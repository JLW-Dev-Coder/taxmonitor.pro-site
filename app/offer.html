How to Proceed

Review the proposal details and select the Tax Monitoring option that best fits your needs. Submitting this proposal confirms your approval of the selected option only.

After proposal approval, a formal Service Agreement and Contract will be generated and presented for electronic signature in the next step.

If any monitored tax year was filed as Married Filing Jointly (MFJ), each spouse must independently enroll and authorize monitoring. Jointly filed years cannot be accessed or monitored unless both spouses complete enrollment.

Services begin only after the Service Agreement is signed and payment is received.

About Pricing

All Tax Monitoring options are offered as one-time service fees. Each option includes a defined service scope and, where applicable, a set monitoring period.

There are no subscriptions or recurring charges unless separately agreed to in writing.
